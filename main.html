McMac Baxter Creek Watershed Alliance Hub

Intro

The Baxter Creek Watershed Alliance (BCWA) is a non-profit, federally incorporated that is newly established in Millbrook, Cavan Monaghan, Ontario. There is a Steering Committee of general members within the Board of Directors which makes up the BCWA. The Steering Committee, Craig Onafrychuk, Graham Whitelaw, David Webster, are non-voting members designated responsibilities while the Board of Directors have voting rights in establishing the direction and by-laws of the Alliance. The Baxter Creek Watershed Alliance is in its preliminary stage therefore we are preparing the data to provide a foundation for future projects. The Baxter Creek Watershed Hub is a multifaceted approach to consolidate baseline data to support a long-term investment in studying the watershed.

Solution

This Collaboration Project with BCWA and Fleming College GIS Department has a foundation in specific learning outcomes for the students involved. There are mandatory approaches to create a successful outcome of the project. These methodologies include preprocessing of data, a creation of a database to ensure ease of access, visualization of important aspects, and enterprise technology to encourage usability within the proposed services. The most valuable workflow for the BCWA is documentation of our steps to allow the framework of these methodologies to potentially be used by other Watershed protection alliances across Ontario to be able to expand their services.
